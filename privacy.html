Privacy Policy

I have become tired of looking at something on one website and then seeing ads for it everywhere I go. So, let's keep it simple. We do not use cookies and we do not track any personally identifiable information. If you send us information, such as your email address when you submit the feedback form, we will use it only for the purpose of communicating with you.

We do ask writers for their name, email, and a short biography to accompany their story if it is accepted. The information is only used when the story is published and we do not include the author's email on the site unless they authorize us to do so.

If you have any questions, contact me. -- Irene P. Smith (editor at nimuesgrotto.com)
